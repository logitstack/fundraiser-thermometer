Features

Animated Thermometer

Liquid-fill animation with wave effects, milestone banners, and confetti at 25%, 50%, 75%, and 100%.

Real-Time Sync

Updates across windows instantly via BroadcastChannel. Enter a donation and the display reacts in under a second.

Offline QR Code

Generates scannable QR codes with no external API. Fully embedded — works with no internet connection.

5 Donation Sources

Track Cash, Check, Pledges, Square, and Website separately with automatic totalling.

Fully Customizable

Change fill color, background, title font and size. Configure side info panels with custom labels.

Offline & Private

Everything runs in your browser. No server, no account, no data sent anywhere. Export backups as JSON.

How to use

1

Open both windows

Click the buttons above to open the Control Panel and Thermometer Display in separate tabs.

2

Project the display

Drag the Thermometer Display to your projector or second screen. Press F to go fullscreen. Press Q to toggle the QR code panel.

3

Configure your campaign

In the Control Panel, set your title, goal amount, and optional info panels. Apply a theme that matches your event branding.

4

Enter donations live

Type an amount into any category and press Enter. The thermometer updates instantly. Celebrations fire automatically at 25%, 50%, 75%, and 100%.
