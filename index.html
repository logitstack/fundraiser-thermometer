How to use

1

Open both windows

Click the buttons above to open the Control Panel and Thermometer Display in separate tabs or windows.

2

Project the display

Drag the Thermometer Display window to your projector or second screen. Press F to go fullscreen. Press Q to toggle the QR code panel.

3

Configure your campaign

In the Control Panel, set your title, goal amount, and optional info panels. Apply a theme that matches your event branding.

4

Enter donations live

Type an amount into any category field and press Enter. The thermometer updates instantly. At 25%, 50%, 75%, and 100% a celebration fires automatically.
